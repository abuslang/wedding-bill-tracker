Dholki / Haldi

The Dholki and Haldi ceremonies are beautiful pre-wedding celebrations that bring families together. The Dholki features traditional songs and music, while the Haldi ceremony involves applying turmeric paste to the bride and groom for a natural glow. These events are filled with joy, laughter, and cultural traditions.
